My story....


After graduating from the University of Oregon with a Bachelor's degree in French (oui, je parle francais!), I found myself working various administrative positions, and through the years I often felt something was missing. After some reflection I decided to make a change. I began my journey by starting a photography business (see my photography portfolio here).

I soon got my first clients and had a small portfolio. Not long after I wanted to build a website for my growing business. I didn't want a cookie cutter site and I wanted to really build it from the bottom up. I soon found online courses and started learning HTML and CSS in my spare time.

It wasn't long before found myself spending a lot of time coding and I discovered that I had an appeitite to learn more. I did some research and decided to go back to school.

I have finished my courses at a local code school in Portland Oregon, my emphasis is on JavaScript, PHP and Drupal. I am looking forward to applying my new skills to the workplace. If you are interested in my professional history please see my LinkedIn profile here.
